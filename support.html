Six ways to make a difference

How you can help

Whether you give time, money, expertise, or equipment — every contribution lands directly in a young person's experience.

💛

Financial donation

Your donation funds camp fees, equipment, and badge programmes. Every rand goes directly to a young person's experience.

What R500 buys — A scout's camp fees for one overnight trip — tent, food, and activities included.
Contact our treasurer →
🏢

Become a sponsor

Sponsoring 1st Henley puts your business in front of a loyal local community all year round.

Sponsorship includes — Website listing, newsletter mention, event signage, and a thank-you certificate.
Enquire about sponsorship →
🙋

Volunteer as a leader

The most powerful thing you can give is your time. No prior Scouting experience needed — we'll train you.

Time commitment — Friday evenings, plus occasional camps. Full SCOUTS SA training provided.
Talk to us about volunteering →
🎒

Donate equipment

If you have outdoor gear gathering dust — tents, sleeping bags, cooking equipment, ropes — we'll put it to work immediately.

Current wishlist — Tents, gas burners, first aid kits, rain gear, and pioneering rope.
Get in touch →
🎪

Fundraising events

From potjie competitions to community markets — our fundraising events are fun for everyone.

Past examples — Thenius Hook Potjie Competition, Lion's Club Treehouse Challenge, community fetes.
