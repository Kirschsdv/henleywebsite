Meerkats and milkshakes — a rainy Friday done right

← Back to all stories
Cubs February 2020 by 1st Henley Scouts

Friday 7 February 2020. Overcast skies, constant rain — and the Meerkats patrol out on their outing at Molly's, milkshakes in hand, entirely unbothered by the weather.

Not every scouting story needs a 40km mountain hike or a pioneered treehouse. Sometimes the story is a group of seven-year-olds sitting around a table on a rainy afternoon, laughing over milkshakes they'd earned through effort and good spirit.

Thank you to Alsabe and Molly's for the arrangements, and to every Meerkat who showed up on a wet Friday and decided the rain wasn't going to stop anything.

Go Meerkats.
