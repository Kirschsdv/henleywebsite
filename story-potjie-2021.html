Potjies, pioneering, and the spirit award — Thenius Hook 2021

← Back to all stories
Scouts October 2021 by Elizabeth Downes

2 October 2021. Early start. Everyone met at the hall to pack equipment, then the scouts walked up the road to the Henley Bowls Club. By 10:00, potjies were on the fire and scouts were busy pioneering.

They built a full-sized camp table and a flagpole — which flew the 1st Henley flag high for all to see. Their competition stall carried the scouts theme with full commitment throughout the day.

At the end of the day, 1st Henley won both the spirit competition and the theme competition. Of course, their theme was Scouts.

A special thank you to parents and Scouters: Otter Katherine Downes, Kestrel Kirschner de Villiers, Panther Lucrecia Haskins, and Wolverine Richard Haskins. And to Serendipity Farm Stall and the Henley Deli for sponsoring the potjie ingredients, and the Henley Bowls Club for sponsoring the entries.

"I hope you all remember how to do square lashings."
