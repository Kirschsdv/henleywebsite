Your first Friday

What to expect when you arrive

No appointment needed — just show up on a Friday. Here's what happens:

  1. Arrive at the Scout Hall — 5 Hearn Road, Henley-on-Klip. Cubs from 17:50, Scouts from 18:50.
  2. Introduce yourself to one of the leaders (look for the uniforms). They'll welcome you and explain the evening's programme.
  3. Your child joins in — no uniform needed, no pressure. They'll be included in whatever the group is doing that evening.
  4. Parents can stay and watch or come back at the end of the session.
  5. Afterwards, chat to a leader about joining formally, the uniform, and fees.
Please WhatsApp or email us before your first visit — just to confirm we're not away on camp that evening.

Parent FAQ

The questions we get asked most before a first visit.

Cubs start at age 7 and move to Scouts at around 11. There's no upper age limit for Scouts — members can stay in the Troop until they're 18 and beyond as leaders.
