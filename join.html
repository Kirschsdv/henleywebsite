When & where

Come and see for yourself

The best way to know if Scouting is right for your child is to come along on a Friday. No forms, no commitment for the first visit — just arrive and we'll show you around. Please WhatsApp us first to confirm we're not away on camp.

Cubs (ages 7–10)Friday evenings, 18:00 – 19:30
Scouts (ages 11–18)Friday evenings, 19:00 – 21:00
