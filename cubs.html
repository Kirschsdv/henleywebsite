Cubs Pack

For boys and girls aged 7–10. Where the adventure begins.

"Cubbing is a fun and safe environment for little people to become bigger little people. We make use of the play-to-learn system that makes learning fun." — Baloo, Pack Scouter

About the Pack

Cubs meet every Friday from 18:00 – 19:30 at the Scout Hall, 5 Hearn Road, Henley-on-Klip. The programme is designed around fun, outdoor skills, friendship, and the award badge system.

Come along to a meeting to see what it's all about — no commitment needed for the first visit. Parents are welcome to stay and watch.
